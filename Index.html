Call for Book Chapters on Brain and Behavior Computing

Introduction

Main purpose of bringing out a book on Brain and Behaviour computing is for scholars, academia, doctors, researchers and industry professional working in the area of development of efficient models for diagnosis and identification of different disorders related to brain. The focus of the book will be on:

  • Fundamentals, models, algorithms related to brain and behavior computing.
  • Implementation methodologies and related algorithms
  • Case Studies and Simulation Examples
  • Automatic identification of brain related disorders
  • Design & simulation, research scope, Issues and Challenges

    The book aims to cover (but not limited to) following:


    1. Introduction and Data Acquisition
    2. Classification and Characteristics of brain Computing
    3. Signal Processing techniques and algorithms in brain behavior analysis
    4. Data Science in Brain Signal computing
    5. Image Processing techniques in Brain and Behavior
    6. Brain simulation tools
    7. Role of statistics in brain and behavior science
    8. Visual brain Computing
    9. Parallel, Distributed and Cloud Computing (PDC) in Brain and Behavior Science
    10. Machine Learning Techniques for Brain and Behavior Computing
    11. Brain Imaging Modalities
    12. Brain-Computer Interface (BCI)
    13. Psychology and Philosophy aspects of BCI
    14. Case studies
    15. Future Scope for brain and behavior analysis
